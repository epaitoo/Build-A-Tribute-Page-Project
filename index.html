Timeline of Dr Kwame Nkrumah

  • 1909 - born in about 1909 in Nkroful, Gold Coast(Modern Day Ghana)
  • 1935 - arrived in New York,USA
  • 1939 - completed a Bachelor of Arts degree in economics and sociology
  • 1942 - He gained a Bachelor of Theology degree from Lincoln in
  • 1945 - Enrolled at the London School of Economics
  • 1947 - Returned to the Gold Coast and joined the United Gold Coast Convention(UGCC) as the General Secretary.
  • 1949 - left the UGCC and formed his own party, Convention People Party(CPP)
  • 1950 - He was arrested by the Colonial Government for a strike which led to violence
  • 1951 - Dr Nkrumah was released from prison. CPP won the first general election and he became the leader of Government Business and Prime Minister
  • 1957 - Ghana became independent
  • 1957 - 1966 - He became the first President Of Ghana and was overthrown on 1966.
  • 1972 - He died of prostate cancer in April at the age of 62. He was buried in a tomb in the village of his birth, Nkroful, Ghana
